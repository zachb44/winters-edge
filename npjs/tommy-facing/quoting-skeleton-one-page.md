# 🧰 QUOTING — GLANCE CARD. DON'T READ IT. SCAN IT.

**Run the 5 steps. Hit a hard question? → the BIG 3 box. Check the FLOOR before you say a number.**
*(Curveball you don't see here? It's on the reference sheet at home — not for live use.)*

---

## ⬇️ THE 5 STEPS (every job, same order)

**1. WHAT SURFACE?** → Driveway · House · Patio/walk *(Wood = "let me look." Roof = "not yet.")*

**2. MEASURE** → Concrete: L × W = sq ft *(1 big step ≈ 3 ft)* · House: match it to a size below

**3. RATE × SIZE = starting number** *(from the box below)*

**4. BIGGER?** → 2 stories **+30–50%** · Heavy growth **+20–40%** · Oil **+$25–75** · Rust **+$25–50**

**5. CHECK THE FLOOR ⬇️, then say it like you mean it.**

---

## 💵 RATES (real Columbia numbers — they're yours)

| SURFACE | PRICE |
|---|---|
| **Driveway** | 1-car **$100–175** · 2-car **$150–250** · 3-car **$225–300** · big **$275–350** *(or $0.20–0.25/sq ft)* |
| **House** ⭐ | under 1,500sf **$200–300** · **1,500–2,500sf = $300–450 ← your target** · 2,500–3,500sf **$400–550** · 3,500+ **$500–600+** |
| **Patio / walkway** | $0.20–0.25/sq ft — **bundle it onto a driveway** |
| **Sidewalk add-on** | **+$50–100** onto a driveway |
| **WOOD / ROOF** | 🚫 Don't quote live. Wood: **"Let me take a quick look."** Roof: **"I don't offer roofs yet."** |

**Bigger by 100 sq ft? → add ~$25–50. Don't re-quote from scratch.**
**Always ask: "Want me to hit the driveway/walkway while I'm here?"** *(bundle = bigger ticket)*

## 🛑 THE FLOOR — read before EVERY number

> ## ⚠️ Below **$50/hr NET for your REAL time (door to door)** = RE-QUOTE OR WALK.

**$60/hr is the TARGET on the meter. ~$50/hr NET is the floor** — *net = after gas + chemicals + wear, on your real door-to-door hours (drive, setup, prep, wash, cleanup).* $60 is not take-home; the floor is what's actually left.

**Quick check before you say a low number:**
**(your price − ~$30 gas/chems) ÷ honest hours = what you ACTUALLY make.**

- House wash $375, ~3.5 hrs → **~$98/hr net ✅ say it**
- 2-car driveway + oil = $250, ~2 hrs → **~$112/hr net ✅ say it**
- Big 2-story, heavy growth, tempted to say $300, really 6 hrs → **~$42/hr net 🛑 NO. Re-quote $550+.**

⚠️ **Not sure how long it'll take? You're about to UNDER-QUOTE. Estimate HIGH.**
**A half-day big job is NOT "a wash." Price the big one as the big one.**

## 🗣️ THE BIG 3 — the questions that freeze you. READ THE ANSWER.

> ### 💰 "HOW MUCH?"
> **"Depends on size and surface — let me measure real quick and I'll give you a number right now."**
> *(Then run the 5 steps. Wood/roof/complex: "Let me take a quick look so I quote it right instead of guessing.")*

> ### 🛡️ "WILL IT DAMAGE MY SIDING / WOOD / WINDOWS?"
> **"No — I match the method to the surface. Your siding gets a soft wash — low pressure and a cleaning solution that does the work, not high pressure. I only use real pressure on concrete."**

> ### 📋 "ARE YOU INSURED?"
> **"I'm owner-operated and careful — I protect your plants, fixtures, and windows before I start. I'm getting my general liability set up now."**
> ⚠️ *Don't claim a policy you don't have. This is the honest line until the real GL is filed.*

## 🚧 ONE GUT CHECK BEFORE YOU SAY YES
**Too big to solo?** 2+ stories you can't reach from the ground · roof · huge commercial flatwork · heavy growth on a big house · need a ladder + wand at once · "can you do it ALL by Saturday?"
**→ Say:** *"That's bigger than I want to rush solo — let me bring a hand or take a real look and quote it right."*
