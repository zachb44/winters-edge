## ⬇️ THE 5 STEPS (every job, same order)

**1. WHAT SURFACE?** → Driveway · House · Patio/walk · Wood *(Roof = "not yet")*

**2. MEASURE** → Concrete: L × W = sq ft *(1 big step ≈ 3 ft)* · House: match it to a size below

**3. RATE × SIZE = starting number** *(from the box below)*

**4. BIGGER?** → 2 stories +30–50% · Heavy growth +20–40% · Oil +$25–75 · Rust +$25–50

**5. CHECK THE FLOOR ⬇️, then say it like you mean it.**

## 🛑 THE FLOOR — read this before EVERY number

> ## ⚠️ Under **$50/hr for your REAL time (door to door)** = RE-QUOTE OR WALK.
> ## Not sure how long it'll take? You're about to UNDER-QUOTE. Estimate HIGH.

**Quick gut check before you say a low number:**
**(your price − ~$30 gas/chems) ÷ honest hours = what you ACTUALLY make.**

- House wash $375, ~3.5 hrs → **~$98/hr ✅ say it**
- 2-car driveway + oil = $250, ~2 hrs → **~$112/hr ✅ say it**
- Big 2-story, heavy growth, you wanna say $300, it's really 6 hrs → **~$42/hr 🛑 NO. Re-quote $550+.**

**A half-day big job is NOT "a wash." Price the big one as the big one.**
