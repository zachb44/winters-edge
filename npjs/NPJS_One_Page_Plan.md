## WHY
To be my own boss, make real money, support myself and help Dad — and to do
honest, quality work in an industry where people have lost trust because of
hack jobs. I'd rather do it right than do it fast and cheap.

## ONE-YEAR VISION (this day next year)
Wake up early. Get in my own truck. Run through my job book and calls in the
morning, then spend the day on job sites — a steady flow of work coming in
through referrals, where I quote jobs confidently and don't get caught off
guard. Fridays are for wrapping up and quoting next week, not starting new.

## FIRST SERVICE LINE (the one bullet)
**Power washing.**
- Bobby's lending me a washer — zero equipment cost to start
- Easiest to quote (priced by surface / sq ft, not guesswork)
- Visible before/after results sell the next job for me
- Can be done out of Dad's car TODAY — no truck required
- Low skill ceiling, fast wins, money this month

> Drywall and window/door painting stay as my "already know how" backup work
> (Jerry's windows, referral jobs). Decks and carpentry are PHASE 2 — they need
> a truck, power tools, and usually two people. Not now.

## FIRST 30 DAYS (do these — nothing else)
1. **Confirm Bobby's power washer works.** Test it this week. Done = it runs.
2. **Get one power-washing job booked and completed.** Driveway, deck, siding —
   anything. Take a before/after photo. (Use Zach's quoting skeleton.)
3. **Finish Jerry's windows** (2 windows + 2 crawl-space doors) after the storms.
4. **Run the same lead play every week:** church on Sunday + hand 5 cards to
   real people + text 3 past contacts. Not "look for leads." This exact action.
5. **Track every dollar in and out** in one place (Zach is setting this up).

## THE NUMBER THAT SAYS IT'S WORKING
First 30 days: **book and complete 4 paid jobs of any size.** Proof of motion,
not a revenue target.
By month 3: a real monthly number once we know the truck payment + bills.
(We still need to nail down your actual monthly bills — that's homework.)

## TRUCK — THE HONEST VERSION
Truck = **after July 4th**, $15K target / $20K ceiling, ~$300/mo cap.
The truck does NOT come first. The jobs come first. Every job you book in the
next 3 weeks is down-payment money AND proof to the lender you have income.
Goal: walk onto the lot July 5 with ~$3K down, not $1K.

## CHECK-IN CADENCE
**Every Sunday, 10 minutes, text or call.** Three questions only:
1. How many jobs did you book/complete this week?
2. What's the cash number right now?
3. What's the one thing stuck?

That's it. No spreadsheets. No reports. Just the three numbers and the blocker.
